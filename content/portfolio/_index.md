## Focus Areas

### Enterprise Governance
PMO stand-up, governance design, stage-gate structures, executive reporting, and project management capability development.

### Portfolio Visibility
Portfolio intake, prioritization, capacity planning, and leadership dashboards that improve decision-making across complex initiatives.

### Delivery Systems
Azure DevOps governance, Power BI reporting, sprint discipline, and delivery metrics that create visibility into execution performance.

### Transformation Programs
Large-scale infrastructure, enterprise systems, and operational modernization initiatives requiring cross-functional leadership.

## Leadership Themes

Across these initiatives, my work consistently centers on:

- building governance before scaling tools
- creating visibility into demand, capacity, and risk
- improving execution through practical delivery discipline
- helping leadership align investment decisions with strategic outcomes
