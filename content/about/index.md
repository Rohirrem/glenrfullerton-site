## About Glen Fullerton

I am an enterprise program and PMO leader with more than 30 years of experience helping organizations turn strategy into reliable execution.

My work focuses on three critical areas of enterprise delivery success.

### Enterprise PMO Development
Designing governance frameworks and portfolio management structures that provide leadership with visibility into complex initiatives while enabling disciplined decision making.

### Enterprise Program Leadership
Leading large cross-functional initiatives across IT infrastructure, application development, supply chain operations, and digital transformation environments.

### Delivery System Modernization
Implementing delivery frameworks and tools that allow organizations to understand demand, capacity, risk, and progress across large portfolios of work.

Over the course of my career I have supported organizations in establishing enterprise PMOs, improving portfolio governance, and leading complex programs that require coordination across multiple business units and technical domains.

My goal is always the same: create the governance and delivery systems that allow leadership teams to reliably turn strategy into results.
