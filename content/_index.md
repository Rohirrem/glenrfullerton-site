## What I Do

### 🏛 Enterprise PMO Development
Establishing enterprise PMO capabilities that enable leadership to align investments with strategy, govern complex initiatives with confidence, and improve delivery consistency across the organization. This includes PMO stand-up, governance framework design, portfolio controls, reporting structures, and project management capability development.

### ⚙ Portfolio Governance & Visibility
Implementing portfolio management processes that give leadership a clear view of demand, capacity, risk, and progress across major initiatives. By introducing disciplined intake processes, prioritization models, and executive reporting dashboards, organizations gain the visibility needed to make informed strategic decisions.

### 🔄 Delivery System Modernization
Improving execution reliability by implementing practical delivery frameworks and governance models within modern tool environments such as Azure DevOps. This includes delivery metrics, capacity planning models, sprint governance, and structured approaches to managing complex cross-team initiatives.
